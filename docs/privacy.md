# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Workout Tracker collects, uses, and stores your information.

*Last updated: 24 March 2026*

## 1. Overview

Workout Tracker is designed to store and manage your personal workout data. Your data is used only to provide the service to you.

We do not sell your data, run ads, or use tracking analytics.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

When you use Workout Tracker, we collect and store the following information.

### Account information

- Email address (used as your login identifier)
- Authentication managed by AWS Cognito

Passwords are never stored directly by the application.

### Workout data

- Exercises
- Sets, reps, and weights
- Workout notes
- Tags
- Body weight entries
- Workout timestamps

### Saved exercises

- Exercise names
- Notes
- Tags

### Subscription data

If you subscribe, we store:
- Subscription status
- Billing period
- Stripe customer ID
- Stripe subscription ID

We do **not** store payment card details. Payments are processed securely by Stripe.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to operate the Workout Tracker service. This includes:

- Authenticating your account
- Storing and syncing workouts across devices
- Maintaining your exercise library
- Managing subscription status
- Enforcing free-tier limits

Workout Tracker does **not**:
- Sell personal data
- Share data for advertising
- Use tracking or analytics services
- Display advertising

---

## 4. Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the **eu-west-2 (London)** region.

Services used include:

- Amazon DynamoDB
- AWS Cognito
- AWS Lambda
- AWS API Gateway

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. DynamoDB encrypts stored data at rest.

---

## 5. Local Device Storage

Workout Tracker is built offline-first.

Some data may be stored locally on your device, including:
- Active workouts
- Pending (unsynced) workouts
- Saved exercises

This data syncs to the cloud when your device reconnects to the internet.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists.

If you request deletion of your account, your data will be removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## 7. Backup & Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored in Workout Tracker.

This includes the use of infrastructure features provided by our hosting provider (AWS), which may include automated backups and recovery capabilities for stored data.

However, no system can guarantee that data will never be lost. While we aim to protect user data, users should not rely on the service as the sole backup location for important records.

We encourage users to periodically export their data if they wish to keep an independent copy.

---

## 8. Data Export

You can request a copy of your data stored in Workout Tracker.

This may include:
- Workout history
- Saved exercises
- Associated workout information linked to your account

To request a data export, contact us using the email listed below. We will provide your data in a commonly used format where reasonably possible.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Request correction of your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Request a copy of your data

To make a request, contact the email listed below.

These services only process data necessary to provide the service.
